Our Branches Across India

Mumbai

Located in the heart of Mumbai, our branch brings the flavors of South India to the bustling city. Known for our authentic Masala Dosa and filter coffee.

Bengaluru

This branch is famous for its Mysore Masala Dosa and coconut chutneys. A popular spot for techies and locals alike.

Chennai

Our flagship branch, located in Chennai, serves traditional Tamil Nadu dishes like Idli, Vada, and Pongal that capture the true flavors of the region.

Hyderabad

Our Hyderabad branch offers a fusion of Andhra flavors with South Indian classics. Try our spicy Andhra Masala Dosa!

Delhi

In the capital city, our branch has become a favorite spot for North Indians looking to enjoy authentic South Indian cuisine. Don't miss our Uthappam varieties.

Pune

This Pune branch is loved for its fusion of Maharashtrian flavors with classic South Indian dishes. Our Sabudana Dosa is a must-try here.

Kolkata

The Kolkata branch brings the flavors of South India to the east. Our Rasam and Bisi Bele Bath are particularly popular among locals.
